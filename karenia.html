EST. 1661

karenia brevis is an aquatic marine organism; red tide.. it’s unicellular, photosynthetic and halophilic.. it blooms in masses; rusting warm, coastal waters.

it’s mixotrophic; living by the graces of apex. it’s attracted to environments indifferent to pollution; apathetically deleting remaining nutrients.. blocking sunlight and releasing a paralysis-inducing fragrance..

it travels freely, using its two flagella, in search of another oasis to bloom..
